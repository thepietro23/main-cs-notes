## 1. Exam at a glance

| Stage | Type | Counts in final merit? |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1 (Prelims)** | Online objective — screening | ❌ Qualifying only |
| **Phase 2 (Mains)** | Objective + Descriptive | ✅ **85%** |
| **Phase 3 (Interview)** | ~50 marks | ✅ **15%** |

**Final merit = 85% Phase 2 + 15% Interview.** Phase 1 sirf gate-pass.

### Phase 1 (Prelims)
| Paper | Subjects | Marks | Time | Qualifying |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 (common) | GA (+financial) · English · Quant · Reasoning | 100 | 60 min | 30% |
| Paper 2 (**IT**) | Information Technology | 100 | 40 min | 40% |

Negative marking: **0.25** per wrong answer. Dono paper back-to-back.

### Phase 2 (Mains) ⭐ *(yahi merit decide karta hai)*
| Paper | Type | Marks | Time | Cutoff | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 | English (Descriptive — drafting skills) | 100 | 60 min | 30% | 1/3 |
| Paper 2 | **IT** — MCQ: logic-flow, debugging, syntax, dry-run output, data analysis | 100 | 180 min | 40% | 2/3 |

Phase 2 aggregate cutoff 50%. Interview shortlist = 3× vacancies.

## 5. Study plan (6-month, CSE revision mode)

| Month | Focus |
|---|---|
| **1** | DBMS + Networks (concept-revise + GFG MCQs) · Quant/Reasoning base · daily 30min GA |
| **2** | OS + Programming/OOP · Quant/Reasoning continue · 1 topic-test/week |
| **3** | DSA + Cyber Security + SW Engg · advanced Quant (DI/puzzles) · start 1 full mock/week |
| **4** | Phase 1 mastery — sectional + full mocks, mistake analysis · GA: SEBI/financial deep-dive |
| **5** | Phase 2 focus — IT deep MCQs (output/debug) + daily descriptive English (1 essay + 1 precis) · all SEBI IT PYQs |
| **6** | Full-length mocks alternate days · final revision (formula sheets, IT short notes, CA) · interview prep |

> **Compressing to 3–4 months?** Merge M1–M2 (IT core in 6 wks), M3 (rest IT + aptitude), M4 (mocks + Phase 2 + descriptive). Sacrifice depth on low-weight subjects, never on top-3.

**Golden rule:** consistency > marathon · har mock ke baad mistake-analysis = sabse important step.
